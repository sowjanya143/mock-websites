# Leadership Data - Investment-Focused Teams

## Overview

All 9 mock financial websites now feature fictitious leadership teams with realistic titles, bios, and investment backgrounds. The data is designed to reflect authentic fund management organizations with proper representation of investment decision-makers.

## FinSpo Collection Criteria Implementation

The leadership data follows the FinSpo investment collection framework:

### Key People of Interest
- **Analysts** (Equity, Fixed Income, Structured Credit, Technology, etc.)
- **Portfolio Managers** (Equities, Fixed Income, Alternatives, Derivatives, etc.)
- **Chief Investment Officers** (CIO)
- **Heads of Divisions** (Equity, Fixed Income, Research, Trading, Alternatives, etc.)
- **Traders** (Equities, Fixed Income, Derivatives, Commodities, etc.)
- **Risk Managers** (Quantitative Risk, Credit Risk, Macro Risk, etc.)
- **Strategists & Economists** (Macro, Quantitative, Market)
- **Senior Executives** with investment oversight (CEO with investment responsibilities, founders)

### Multiple Roles
Several team members have multiple roles reflecting real organizational structures:
- **Founder & Chief Investment Officer** (primary investment decision-maker)
- **Chief Executive Officer & Head of Investments** (responsible for fund management)
- **Chief Strategist & Head of Research** (combined strategic/research role)

### Exclusions
Team members NOT included (not directly involved in fund management):
- Chief Financial Officer (unless also Head of Investments)
- Chief Operating Officer (unless also investment-related)
- Chief Risk Officer (excluded - compliance/risk, not investment)
- General Counsel (excluded - legal)
- Human Resources executives
- Technology/IT executives (unless also investment role like Quantum's CTO equivalent)

## Data Quality

### Characteristics Implemented

1. **Realistic Names** - Diverse global names reflecting multinational financial institutions
2. **Detailed Titles** - Specific investment roles matching FinSpo collection criteria
3. **Biographical Depth** - 100-150 character bios with:
   - Years of experience
   - Asset sizes managed
   - Key expertise areas
   - Notable achievements
   - Specializations
4. **Realistic Hierarchies** - Clear reporting structures with:
   - C-level executives (CEO, CIO, Chief Economist)
   - Division heads (Head of Equities, Head of Research)
   - Portfolio managers (managing $B+ in assets)
   - Analysts and traders (specialists in specific areas)
5. **Varied Backgrounds** - Different academic credentials (PhDs, MBAs), tenures, and specializations

The leadership data appears on the `/leadership` route for each site:

- **Sentinel**: `/leadership` - 10 investment professionals with pagination (5 per page)
- **Apex**: `/leadership` - 10 investment professionals with pagination
- **Meridian**: `/leadership` - 10 investment professionals with pagination (optional cookie banner)
- **Premier**: `/leadership` - 10 investment professionals with pagination
- **Zenith**: `/leadership` - 10 investment professionals with pagination (CAPTCHA on data pages)
- **Fortis**: `/leadership` - 10 investment professionals (CAPTCHA-protected)
- **Nexus**: `/leadership` - 10 investment professionals (bot detection)
- **Quantum**: `/leadership` - 10 investment professionals (IP-based blocking)
- **Cipher**: `/leadership` - 10 investment professionals (CAPTCHA + content obfuscation)

### Template Integration

Leadership data is loaded from `{site}/data/team.json` and:
1. Processed by `generate_team()` utility function
2. Paginated at 5 members per page
3. Passed to `leadership.html` template
4. Displayed with name, title, and bio
5. Pagination controls for browsing
