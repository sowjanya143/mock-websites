Global Office Locations

North America

Our largest region with offices in New York, Toronto, Los Angeles, Houston, Chicago, and Miami. North America represents 45% of our AUM with focus on gateway markets and emerging metros.

Europe

Strong presence across London, Frankfurt, Paris, Madrid, and Amsterdam. European investments focus on core-plus and value-add opportunities in primary business districts and suburban markets.

Asia-Pacific

Rapidly expanding region with offices in Singapore, Hong Kong, Tokyo, Sydney, Mumbai, and Shanghai. Focus on high-growth markets with urbanization and development tailwinds.

Emerging Markets

Strategic presence in Latin America, Middle East, and Africa with offices in Mexico City, São Paulo, Dubai, Cairo, and Johannesburg. Targeting high-growth emerging markets.

Core Values

Integrity

We operate with absolute transparency and maintain the highest ethical standards. Our investors trust us to act in their interests with unwavering integrity.

Excellence

We pursue excellence in every aspect of our work, from deal sourcing and underwriting to property management and investor relations.

Partnership

We view every investor as a true partner. Your success is our success, and we're committed to delivering results that exceed expectations.

Innovation

We embrace innovation through technology, sustainability initiatives, and new market opportunities that create competitive advantages for our investors.

Stewardship

We're stewards of our investors' capital and custodians of the properties we manage, ensuring long-term value creation for all stakeholders.

Community

We invest in the communities where we operate, creating positive social impact through job creation, affordable housing, and environmental sustainability.
