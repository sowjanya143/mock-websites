About Landmark

60 years of transforming real estate

Our 60-Year Journey

1966

Founding

Robert Heitman founds Landmark Property Advisors with vision to transform commercial real estate investment through disciplined underwriting and active management.

1975

First Fund Launch

Launch of inaugural Landmark Real Estate Fund I with $500M in commitments, establishing foundation for institutional investor relationships.

1990

International Expansion

Establish Landmark offices in London and Tokyo, beginning global real estate investment platform with local market expertise.

2000

AUM Milestone: $10B

Landmark achieves $10B in assets under management across expanded fund complex and investment strategies.

2010

Strategic Diversification

Launch of Private Debt and Public Equity platforms, creating integrated real estate investment company with three complementary strategies.

2015

Sustainability Leadership

Launch green properties fund with commitment to carbon-neutral real estate and LEED certifications across portfolio.

2020

Resilience & Growth

Navigate real estate markets through pandemic, deploy $2.3B capital and establish resilient real estate platform for long-term growth.

2026

Global Leadership

Landmark reaches $47B AUM with 10 global offices, 847 managed properties, and 60-year track record of superior returns.
