Sustainability & ESG

Environmental, Social, and Governance leadership in real estate

Our ESG Commitment

Landmark Property Advisors is committed to integrating environmental, social, and governance principles throughout our real estate platform. We believe sustainable properties generate superior risk-adjusted returns while creating positive impact for investors and communities.

Environmental Leadership

Carbon Reduction

35% Reduction

Landmark portfolio has achieved 35% carbon reduction through energy efficiency improvements, renewable energy adoption, and building system optimization.

Green Certifications

80% LEED

Over 80% of Landmark managed properties have achieved LEED certification (Gold or Platinum), demonstrating commitment to green building standards.

Energy Efficiency

40% Savings

Smart building technologies and energy management systems have reduced energy consumption by average 40% across portfolio properties.

Water Conservation

30% Reduction

Water-efficient fixtures, landscape optimization, and smart irrigation systems reduce water usage by 30% across managed properties.

Social Impact

Community Engagement

Landmark properties support local communities through job creation, affordable housing preservation, and community development initiatives. $25M+ annual investment in community programs.

Workforce Development

Training and development programs supporting property workers, service providers, and local employment. Partnership with community colleges and vocational programs.

Diversity & Inclusion

Landmark is committed to diverse and inclusive real estate platform. Women and underrepresented minorities comprise 45% of investment team and 50% of board leadership.

Health & Wellness

Portfolio properties prioritize tenant health and wellbeing through biophilic design, air quality management, and wellness programs.

Governance & Accountability

Board Oversight

ESG principles overseen by dedicated board committee with quarterly reporting on environmental, social, and governance metrics.

Transparency & Reporting

Annual ESG report measuring progress against established targets and benchmarks. Third-party verification of sustainability claims.

Investor Alignment

Investor agreements include ESG performance metrics and reporting requirements aligned with global sustainability standards.

Supply Chain Responsibility

Requirements for vendors, contractors, and service providers to meet ESG standards and demonstrate sustainable business practices.
