Sustainability & ESG Leadership

Our Sustainability Initiatives

LEED Certification & Green Buildings

We prioritize LEED certification across our property portfolio. Currently, 65% of our properties are LEED certified or pursuing certification, significantly reducing energy consumption and operational costs while providing healthier environments for tenants.

Carbon Reduction & Climate Action

We've achieved 35% carbon reduction across our portfolio through renewable energy adoption, building efficiency upgrades, and operational improvements. Our target is 50% reduction by 2030 and carbon neutrality by 2040.

Community Impact & Social Equity

We're committed to creating positive community impact through affordable housing initiatives, local job creation, and community development programs. 25% of new developments include affordable housing components.

Water Conservation & Waste Reduction

We've implemented comprehensive water conservation programs and waste reduction initiatives across all properties. Water usage per square foot has been reduced by 28%, and we've achieved 60% waste diversion rates.

ESG Targets & Metrics

Environmental

50% by 2030
Carbon reduction target across portfolio. Current progress: 35% achieved. Focus areas include renewable energy and building efficiency.

Social

25% Affordable
Affordable housing target in new developments. Creating inclusive communities with 1,500+ affordable units delivered to date.

Governance

50% Women
Gender diversity target in leadership. Current: 42% women in management. Committed to achieving parity in senior roles by 2027.

Creating Sustainable Value

Sustainability is not just a corporate initiative at Landmark—it's embedded in our investment philosophy and operational practices. We believe that sustainable properties create superior long-term returns while reducing risk and creating positive environmental and social impact. By integrating ESG principles across our platform, we're building a more resilient, equitable, and sustainable real estate portfolio for our investors and the communities we serve.
