We provided $185M in construction financing for a 2.4 million SF mixed-use development in a major gateway market. The transaction involved complex structural elements including retail, office, residential, and hotel components, requiring sophisticated underwriting and ongoing credit monitoring.

Our construction financing expertise enabled us to navigate the complex development timeline, coordinate with multiple subcontractors, and make proactive loan modifications as market conditions evolved. We maintained disciplined loan-to-value ratios (max 75% LTC) and required comprehensive contractor insurance and performance guarantees.

The project stabilized on schedule, and we realized a 7.8% yield over the 4-year construction and lease-up period before the sponsor executed a permanent take-out financing, generating strong returns with minimal credit losses.
