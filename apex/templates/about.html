About Apex Investment Group

Company History

Founded in 1998 by James Montgomery, Apex Investment Group has grown to become a leading global alternative asset manager. Over 28 years, we have evolved from a single real estate fund to a $450 billion multi-strategy platform managing investments across real estate, private equity, credit, and opportunistic strategies.

Our journey reflects a commitment to disciplined investing, operational excellence, and long-term value creation. Through multiple market cycles including the 2008 financial crisis, the 2020 pandemic, and various geopolitical challenges, we have consistently delivered superior risk-adjusted returns to our investors while maintaining unwavering ethical standards.

Our Mission

To be the premier global alternative asset manager by delivering exceptional risk-adjusted returns through disciplined investment strategies, deep market expertise, and an unwavering commitment to transparency and integrity with our investors, partners, and stakeholders.

Business Overview

Apex operates across four principal investment strategies, each managed by world-class investment teams with decades of combined experience:

  • Real Estate ($180B): Core-plus and value-add commercial properties, industrial logistics, and infrastructure assets across North America, Europe, and Asia Pacific
  • Private Equity ($140B): Mid-market leveraged buyouts, growth equity, and emerging market growth capital investments
  • Credit & Debt ($80B): Senior and subordinated debt, structured credit, CLO strategies, and direct lending opportunities
  • Opportunistic ($50B): Special situations, distressed opportunities, and cross-asset strategies in dynamic market conditions

Our diversified platform, spanning 12 global offices and 420 investment professionals, enables us to capitalize on market opportunities while maintaining sophisticated risk management and operational excellence across all strategies.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We maintain the highest ethical standards in all business activities
  • Excellence: We pursue exceptional performance in every investment
  • Innovation: We continuously develop new investment approaches
  • Transparency: We communicate openly with all stakeholders
  • Accountability: We take full responsibility for our actions and outcomes
