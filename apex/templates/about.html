About Apex Asset Management

Company History

Founded in 1994, Apex Asset Management has established itself as a leading global multi-asset manager with $450 billion in assets under management. Over 32 years, we have built a trusted platform serving institutional investors, family offices, pension funds, and retail clients across Asia Pacific, Americas, and Europe.

Our Asia-centric heritage, combined with global expertise, positions us uniquely to identify compelling investment opportunities across emerging and developed markets. Through multiple market cycles, we have consistently delivered strong risk-adjusted returns while maintaining our commitment to innovation, integrity, and client service excellence.

Our Mission

To be the trusted global asset manager delivering superior investment solutions and risk-adjusted returns through deep market expertise, innovative strategies, and unwavering commitment to client success and responsible investing practices.

Business Overview

Apex operates across four principal asset classes with investment solutions tailored to institutional and retail client needs:

  • Equities ($180B): Asia-focused, emerging markets, and global equity strategies targeting growth and income generation
  • Fixed Income ($140B): Government and corporate bonds, high-yield credit, and multi-asset income solutions globally
  • Alternatives ($80B): Real assets, infrastructure, private equity, and hedge fund strategies
  • Multi-Asset ($50B): Balanced funds and target-date strategies for diversified portfolio construction

Our diversified platform spans 28 global offices with 2,400 employees including 850 investment professionals and 420 research analysts. We serve 2,800+ institutional investors and 4,500+ retail clients with investment solutions designed to meet their long-term financial objectives.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We maintain the highest ethical standards in all business activities
  • Excellence: We pursue exceptional performance in every investment
  • Innovation: We continuously develop new investment approaches
  • Transparency: We communicate openly with all stakeholders
  • Accountability: We take full responsibility for our actions and outcomes
