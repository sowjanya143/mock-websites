About Heitman Capital Management

Company History

Heitman Capital Management was founded to provide leading alternative investment solutions across real estate, private equity, and debt strategies. With over 25 years of experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted alternative asset manager managing approximately $450 billion in assets across global markets.

Our success is built on deep market expertise, disciplined investment processes, and a commitment to delivering exceptional risk-adjusted returns to our investors. Through various market environments, we have maintained our focus on identifying compelling investment opportunities and creating long-term value.

Our Mission

To be a premier global alternative asset manager by delivering superior returns to our investors through disciplined investment strategies, operational excellence, and unwavering commitment to integrity and transparency.

Business Overview

Heitman operates across four principal investment strategies:

  • Real Estate: $180B in commercial properties, residential assets, and mixed-use developments globally
  • Private Equity: $140B in leveraged buyouts and growth capital investments
  • Debt: $80B in corporate debt, structured finance, and credit opportunities
  • Opportunistic: $50B in special situations and cross-asset opportunities

Our diversified platform enables us to capitalize on market dislocations while maintaining sophisticated risk management practices.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We maintain the highest ethical standards in all business activities
  • Excellence: We pursue exceptional performance in every investment
  • Innovation: We continuously develop new investment approaches
  • Transparency: We communicate openly with all stakeholders
  • Accountability: We take full responsibility for our actions and outcomes
