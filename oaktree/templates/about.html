About Oaktree Capital Management

Our Story

Oaktree Capital Management was founded in 1995 with the vision of bringing professional capital to inefficient markets. With $290 billion in global assets under management, Oaktree has grown into one of the world's leading global alternative asset managers.

Our investment philosophy emphasizes rigorous analysis, thorough due diligence, and disciplined capital deployment. We focus on identifying opportunities where we can apply our expertise to generate superior risk-adjusted returns for our investors.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our dealings.
  • Expertise: Our team brings decades of experience in credit and distressed investing.
  • Innovation: We continuously evolve our strategies to adapt to changing markets.
  • Performance: We are focused on delivering superior returns for our investors.
  • Responsibility: We emphasize strong governance and risk management.

Global Presence

Oaktree operates globally with offices in major financial centers across North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific. Our international presence enables us to identify and execute on investment opportunities worldwide while maintaining close client relationships.
