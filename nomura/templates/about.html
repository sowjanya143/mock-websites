About Nomura

Nomura Inc. is one of the world's leading global investment banks with deep expertise in equities, fixed income, derivatives, and alternative investments. With a heritage spanning over a century, Nomura serves institutional clients worldwide with sophisticated financial solutions and market insights.

Company Overview

Global Presence

Operating in 30+ countries with offices across major financial centers including Tokyo, New York, London, Hong Kong, and Singapore.

Expertise

Comprehensive capabilities spanning equities, fixed income, derivatives, FX, and alternative investments with specialized teams in each area.

Client Focus

Serving institutional investors, pension funds, endowments, corporations, and governments with tailored solutions and advisory services.

Innovation

Leveraging technology and innovation to develop cutting-edge financial solutions and enhance client experiences continuously.
