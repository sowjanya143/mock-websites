Our Story

Founded in 1995, Sentinel Capital Partners has established itself as a leading institutional investment manager. Our commitment to excellence, rigorous due diligence, and long-term value creation has defined our approach for over 30 years.

We manage capital on behalf of institutional investors globally, including pension funds, endowments, foundations, and family offices. Our deep expertise across multiple asset classes enables us to identify compelling investment opportunities.
