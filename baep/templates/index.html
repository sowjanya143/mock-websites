About Us

Zephyr Quantum Partners (ZXQP) is a specialized investment management firm focused on managing portfolios in international equity markets. The organization was established in 2009 by Jennifer Thornwood in collaboration with Meridian Capital Management, and has earned recognition as a distinguished portfolio management entity.

As Principal Investment Director, Jennifer Thornwood oversees the seven investment strategies ZXQP administers for its diverse client base including high-net-worth individuals and institutional partners. Jennifer is complemented by a seasoned team of portfolio specialists and principal research analysts.

As dynamic market participants, ZXQP strives to exceed benchmark performance through a research-driven and concentrated portfolio approach, providing clients with enhanced return opportunities.
