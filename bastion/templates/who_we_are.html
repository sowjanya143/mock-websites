About Bastion Investment Group

Bastion Investment Group is a premier global alternative investment manager with $55 billion in assets under management. Founded on principles of disciplined investing, operational excellence, and long-term value creation, Bastion has established itself as a trusted partner for institutional investors, pension funds, endowments, and high-net-worth families worldwide.

Our success is built on a foundation of deep expertise, rigorous analytical processes, and a team of experienced investment professionals dedicated to delivering superior risk-adjusted returns across multiple alternative asset classes.
